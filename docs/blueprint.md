# **App Name**: RentoVerse

## Core Features:

- User Accounts & Profiles: Enable users to create accounts, log in, and manage their basic landlord or renter profiles. Easy to create login data, should provide whatsapp no.
- Room Listing Creation: Allow landlords to easily post new room listings with details such as precise location, nearest communication option like rail station, bus stop etc. availability of wifi, inverter, ac, water supply condition, monthly rent, and multiple photo uploads.
- Intuitive Room Search & Browse: Provide a comprehensive search interface for renters to find rooms, featuring filters for location, price range, amenities, and an Agoda-like visual layout for browsing listings.
- AI Social Post Composer Tool: An AI-powered tool to automatically draft engaging and relevant social media posts (for Facebook and WhatsApp groups) based on newly added room listings or specified room search requests.
- Automated Social Sharing: Enable one-click or automated sharing of AI-generated social media posts to configured Facebook and WhatsApp channels upon successful listing creation or search request submission.
- Direct Inquiry & Contact: Facilitate direct communication between interested renters and landlords, either by displaying contact details or a simple in-app inquiry form.
- Online Legal Form: In case of a deal done, an online legal form will be given to create trust to comply condition for both.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light and airy color scheme, designed for a modern and inviting feel.
- The color theme will be green, yellow, and red based.
- Headline Font: 'Space Grotesk', a modern sans-serif with a crisp, tech-inspired aesthetic, ideal for capturing attention and presenting information clearly. Its distinctive character adds a contemporary edge to titles and prominent text.
- Body Text Font: 'Inter', a highly readable and versatile grotesque-style sans-serif. Chosen for its clean lines and excellent legibility across various screen sizes, ensuring comfortable reading of listings and descriptions.
- Employ a set of minimalist, line-based icons for navigation, filters, amenities, and user actions. Icons should be clear, intuitive, and complement the clean interface, enhancing user comprehension without distraction.
- Adopt a clean, grid-based layout inspired by Agoda.com, prioritizing visual hierarchy and intuitive information display. Ensure a prominent search bar, easily digestible listing cards with key details, and responsive design for optimal viewing across devices.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful animations for transitions between pages, loading states, and interactive elements. These micro-interactions should feel swift and responsive, guiding the user's eye and enhancing the overall fluidity of the experience.
